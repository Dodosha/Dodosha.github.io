Composition of the group

Attila Dorn

The stage name of Romanian-German singer Carsten Brill, the lead singer of the power heavy metal band Powerwolf.

Charles Greywolf

Real name - David Vogt is a German musician, best known for playing in the power heavy metal band Powerwolf, producer and founder of the recording studio "Studio Greywolf".

Christian Jost

German musician. He is best known as the keyboardist of power metal band Powerwolf.

Matthew Greywolf

Real name -Benjamin Buss is a German heavy metal guitarist. He plays in the band Powerwolf. Participated in such collectives as Red AIM, Flowing Tears. He is a close friend of Charles Greywolf, with whose help, despite the lack of kinship, he adopted the stage name "Greywolf".

Stefan Gemballa

Stefan Gemballa is a German musician known mostly for his work as a drummer for the power metal band Powerwolf.
